About Us

Who we are

Sharavathi Enterprises is a leading multi-brand paint store in Shivamogga, Karnataka. For more than 40 years, we have been transforming the homes and offices in Karnataka with paints from leading brands Asian Paints, Berger Paints, Nippon Paint and Vencer Paints. In fact, we are the No.1 Nippon Paint dealer in the state - a badge of honor that we humbly wear. But what makes us a name to reckon with is not just the brands, but our commitment towards quality and our customers. We know the power of colors- the effect they have on human psychology. Colors go beyond enhancing the aesthetics of a space to breathe a new life into a property. Equipped with a strong knowledge for colors and technical expertise in painting, we provide our customers with end-to-end paint solutions, a wide range of high-quality interior & exterior colors and wood finishes to choose from.

Come, let’s enhance your workplace and your home with positive vibes with our durable-yet-soothing paints from our multi-brand store.
