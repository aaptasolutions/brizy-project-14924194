Protect your building from dampness. Build an impenetrable barrier between the foundation, roof, and walls and unwanted moisture. Explore a wide range of waterproofing solutions from top brands offering cementitious waterproofing, liquid waterproofing, bituminous coating, and more.

Enhance your home and workspace with textured walls. Texture paints are stylish, and add a unique touch to the walls. A wood coating is a lifeguard to any wooden surface protecting it from various natural and man-made stresses, prolonging its longevity. Using wood coatings also makes the surfaces easier to clean. Choose our wood coatings from top and trusted brands for attractive, durable wooden assets.
